Information Stored

We collect information about you when you use the Service for a variety of reasons in order to support ChipsBot and to enable our development team to continue to create safe and satisfying experiences for our users. The information you present us is stored in various ways.

Short-Term Storage

Information we obtain from explicit use, such as invocation of commands, is stored in short-term storage in the form of memory (RAM).
The information residing in RAM is cleared every process restart and cannot be accessed from the outside world except through a direct, authenticated connection to the host servers (secure).

Long-Term Storage

Other information, such as logs are stored in the form of text files.
Log files cannot be accessed from the outside world except through a direct, authenticated connection to the host servers (secure).
Information designed to be customisable, such as Command "prefixes," are stored in Databases.
Database files cannot be accessed from the outside world unless a direct connection is made to certain ports on our host servers.
Any sensitive data, including OAuth flow session tokens (a method of authenticating users) from Discord account logins, are hashed (a process that protects or anonymizes data by storing data under a secure, randomly generated unique id) with industry standard technology to safeguard your data from unauthorized use and disclosure, and to prohibit possible security breaches.
Unfortunately, no website, computer system, or online communication is completely secure. We cannot guarantee that unauthorized access, hacking, data loss, or other breaches will never occur. Your use of our Services is at your own risk.
Unless otherwise specified, whenever you make a purchase, your financial and other transaction-related information will only be used to process your transaction and will not be stored on our servers or used for marketing purposes.
Data will be maintained, processed and stored in the United States of America, Canada, or France, or other jurisdictions, as necessary, for proper delivery of the services or as required by law.
